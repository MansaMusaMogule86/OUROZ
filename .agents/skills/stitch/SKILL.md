\# Skill: Stitch Design System (OUROZ)



\## Objective

Convert high-fidelity Moroccan luxury designs into production-ready React/HTML code while preserving material depth, grain, and lighting.



\## Mandatory Design Tokens

\- \*\*Surface\*\*: `backdrop-filter: blur(20px); background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.25);`

\- \*\*Texture\*\*: Always overlay `url('sandpaper-grain.png')` at 5% opacity.

\- \*\*Lighting\*\*: Apply a 1px white border (`rgba(255,255,255,0.4)`) to all cards to create the "Halo" effect.

\- \*\*Colors\*\*: Sahara Sand (#E5D3B3), Imperial Red (#913023), Zellige Green (#2D4B43).



\## Implementation Logic

Before generating any component, Stitch must analyze the lighting in `brand-entry.jpg` to ensure the "Atlas" atmosphere is maintained.
